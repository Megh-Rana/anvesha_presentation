The Crossroads Dilemma

What’s the Challenge?

Choosing a career path is a monumental decision, yet students often face a confusing abundance of options with a significant lack of personalized guidance. This leads to stress, confusion, and a feeling of being ill-equipped for the future.

What Exists Today?

Existing resources often fall short in providing the precise, accessible, and personalized support students truly need.

Traditional Counseling

Time-consuming, expensive, and often lacks the scalability to support broad student populations effectively.

Online Aptitude Tests

Provide generic, non-adaptive results with limited real-world applicability or deep personalization.

Career Blogs/Forums

Cause information overload and are difficult to distill into actionable insights for individual students.

Introducing Anvesha

An innovative AI-powered platform designed to demystify career choices for students, offering precise and personalized recommendations.

How Anvesha Works

1. User Login

Secure and straightforward access to the Anvesha platform.

2. Takes Survey

A dynamic 21-question survey designed to capture key attributes.

3. AI Model Processes

Advanced AI algorithms analyze survey data to find optimal matches.

4. Generates Recommendation

Provides a primary stream recommendation with a confidence score.

What’s Next?

Our vision extends far beyond current capabilities, aiming to build a comprehensive ecosystem for career development.

College Suggestions

AI-driven recommendations for colleges based on student profiles.

AI Learning Platform

Personalized courses and adaptive skill-building learning paths.

Scholarship Alerts

Real-time alerts about verified scholarships and scam prevention.

Community Discussion

Share experiences about colleges, courses, and job interviews.
